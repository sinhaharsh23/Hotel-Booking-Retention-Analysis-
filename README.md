# Hotel-Booking-Retention-Analysis-
EDA, customer segmentation &amp; cancellation prediction using hotel booking data.



---

## Problem Statement
Hotels suffer major revenue loss due to **~40% booking cancellation rate** and
unoptimised room pricing. This project analyses 10,000 hotel booking records
to predict cancellations and recommend dynamic pricing strategies.

---

## Key Business Insights
1. **Lead time** is the strongest cancellation predictor
2. **Non-refundable** bookings cancel less than 5% of the time
3. **August** is peak — room prices can be 30% higher than January
4. **Online TA** segment has the highest cancellation risk
5. **Corporate** travelers are most loyal and highest value
6. **Special requests** signal high commitment — ask for them at booking
7. **Book Direct** campaign can cut OTA dependency and cancellations

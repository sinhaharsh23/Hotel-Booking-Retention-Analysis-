EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
We analysed 10,000 hotel booking records (City Hotel & Resort Hotel,
2016-2019). Overall cancellation rate is 40%, causing significant revenue
leakage. Using EDA and ML (Decision Tree), we identified key cancellation
drivers and seasonal patterns to guide retention and pricing strategy.

================================================================================
KEY BUSINESS INSIGHTS
================================================================================

INSIGHT 1: HIGH CANCELLATION RATE (40%)
  Nearly 2 in 5 bookings cancel — serious revenue risk.
  City Hotels cancel 8% more than Resort Hotels.
  ACTION: City Hotel retention programs need immediate attention.

INSIGHT 2: LEAD TIME IS THE #1 CANCELLATION PREDICTOR
  Bookings made 150+ days in advance cancel at 2x the rate.
  Long lead time = more time to find alternatives or change plans.
  ACTION: Send re-confirmation emails at Day-60 and Day-30 before arrival.

INSIGHT 3: NON-REFUNDABLE DEPOSITS = NEAR-ZERO CANCELLATION
  No Deposit bookings cancel ~40%. Non-Refundable cancel <5%.
  This is the single most powerful tool to reduce cancellations.
  ACTION: Offer non-refundable rate at 10-15% discount.

INSIGHT 4: AUGUST IS PEAK — DYNAMIC PRICING OPPORTUNITY
  August has highest bookings AND highest ADR.
  January-February are weakest months.
  ACTION: +25% price in Jul-Sep, -15% promo in Jan-Feb.

INSIGHT 5: ONLINE TA = HIGHEST CANCELLATION RISK
  OTA customers (Booking.com etc.) cancel most often.
  No direct relationship with hotel reduces loyalty.
  ACTION: Launch Book Direct campaign with 12% discount.

INSIGHT 6: CORPORATE = HIGHEST VALUE SEGMENT
  Corporate travelers have lowest cancellation rate.
  Most loyal, most predictable, highest lifetime value.
  ACTION: Corporate Loyalty Program with dedicated account manager.

INSIGHT 7: SPECIAL REQUESTS = ENGAGEMENT & LOYALTY SIGNAL
  Guests with 2+ special requests cancel far less.
  Special requests = emotional investment in the stay.
  ACTION: Ask for preferences at booking (room view, meals, etc.).

================================================================================
STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS
================================================================================

For Revenue Manager:
  - Implement seasonal pricing: +25% Jul-Sep, -15% Jan-Feb
  - Use ML model to flag high-risk bookings for follow-up
  - Track RevPAR weekly (not just ADR) for true health view

For Marketing Director:
  - Target long-lead-time + no-deposit + OTA guests with retention emails
  - Book Direct campaign with exclusive perks
  - Corporate loyalty program — highest LTV segment

For Hotel Operations:
  - Pre-arrival call for bookings made 90+ days in advance
  - Train front desk to upsell packages at booking time
  - Maintain waitlist for peak periods

ML MODEL SUMMARY
================================================================================
Best Model    : Decision Tree Classifier
Key Metric    : Recall (minimise false negatives = undetected cancellations)
Top Predictors: 1. lead_time  2. adr  3. deposit_type
Recommendation: Integrate model into booking system.
               Flag bookings with predicted cancel probability > 60%.
               Trigger automated retention workflow for flagged bookings.
